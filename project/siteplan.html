Site Purpose

The main goal of your website, which is to showcase your education, experience, and personal projects to potential employers for seeking employment or internships.

Scenarios

  1. Experience and Skills Showcase
    • What technologies and frameworks do you specialize in?
    • Can you demonstrate proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript?
    • Do you have experience with modern frontend libraries and frameworks like React, Vue.js, Angular, etc.?
  2. Project Demonstrations
    • What types of projects have you worked on?
    • Can you showcase projects that demonstrate your frontend development skills (e.g., responsive design, interactivity, performance optimization)?
    • Are there examples of projects that highlight your creativity and problem-solving abilities?
  3. Design and UX/UI Considerations
    • How do you approach design challenges?
    • Can you explain your thought process behind design decisions on your projects?
    • Do you prioritize accessibility, usability, and responsive design in your work?
  4. Code Quality and Practices
    • Is your code clean, well-structured, and maintainable?
    • Do you follow best practices in frontend development (e.g., modular CSS, JavaScript optimization, version control)?
    • Are you familiar with testing frameworks and methodologies for frontend applications?
  5. Professionalism and Communication
    • Is your site well-organized and easy to navigate?
    • Do you provide clear and concise information about your background, skills, and career goals?
    • Are there ways for potential employers to easily contact you or view your resume/CV?
  6. Adaptability and Learning Orientation
    • Are you open to learning new technologies and adapting to changes in frontend development trends?
    • Can you demonstrate continuous learning through personal projects, certifications, or contributions to the frontend community?
